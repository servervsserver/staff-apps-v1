SvS Team Sign-Up

Hi, thanks for considering signing up to the Server vs Server staff team! Since the scope of this project is so large, the team behind it needs to be too- as there are a lot of different ways to contribute and help out, the plan is to assign certain responsibilities to different teams, listed below.

This form registers your interest in joining the overall SvS team as well as indicating your personal skills and interests that you'd like to put to use as part of it. You should gain access to staff channels not long after you've submitted (if you experience any issues with the form please contact an existing staff member).

Remember, your submitted form will be visible to the rest of the SvS staff team as a reference for your skills, interests and practical information such as your timezone. You can be as detailed or as brief as you like- just note down what you think is helpful to know about you as a member of the team!

  • Moderation - {{jobs["mod"].desc}} expand_more
    • Keeping things clean on the server and the stream
